Who We Are
Rooted in Knowledge,
Guided by Faith
98% Pass Rate 2024

Named after the sacred Cave of Hira where knowledge first descended, Ghar Hira School has been a beacon of learning in Accra since 2010. We offer a rich curriculum that weaves together national academic standards and Islamic education — forming students who excel both in the classroom and in character.

Our dedicated teachers, modern facilities, and vibrant community create an environment where curiosity thrives and every student is seen, supported, and challenged to reach their full potential.
